Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

Black Core Sentinel ("we", "us", or "our") operates the BCS - Audio Recorder Chrome Extension. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Data Collection and Use

We DO NOT collect, store, or require any personal information (such as email addresses or user profiles) during the operation of the extension. All your 10-band EQ presets and application settings are saved purely locally on your device.

2. Local Processing (Audio Privacy)

The core functionality of BCS - Audio Recorder (audio capture and transcoding to WAV/MP3) is performed ENTIRELY LOCALLY within your browser. We do not transmit, store, or monitor your audio recordings on any external servers.

3. Data Protection

Because we do not collect any personal data, your privacy is fully protected by design. We have no data to sell, trade, or transfer to third parties. You have absolute control over your local settings and recordings.

4. User Feedback (Uninstallation Survey)

When you uninstall the extension, you may be redirected to a voluntary, anonymous Google Form survey. This survey is designed to help us improve Black Core Sentinel.

The feedback you provide is used solely for development purposes and contains no personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide it within the open-ended fields.
